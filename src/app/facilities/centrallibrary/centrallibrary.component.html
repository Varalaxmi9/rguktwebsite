Central Library

RGUKT library was started in November 2008. It was started with 740 volumes with 2000 students and 50 members staff. Now today January 31st 2017 it comprises of 1.1 lakh volumes & 1125 titles, with 6000 students and 350 staff. Library is the stepping stone for learning every aspectof our life.

Today, the advent of information technology has resulted in reducing the size of libraries.In fact, these smaller modern libraries are rich potential of information. It has been possible due to the digitization of information. The digital and electronic information is based on digitized data information, which has gradually replaced paper-based records. As the visual information system in comparison to text based information system is getting more and more popular these days, the traditional libraries are becoming hybrid libraries as they are in the process of doing digitization of their documents and moving towards to become digital libraries.

Objectives
  • To procure library as basic requirement to enriching quality education
  • To provide high collection includes Text Books, Reference Books, Newspapers, Journals and Magazines etc. for the users;
  • Encouraging self-learning among the students;
  • Keeping the library in the late hours at the exam periods;
  • To procure books on personality development etc.,
  • To ensure library support with the help of library automation and automated services.
  • To provide Electronic Resources viz., e-Books, e-Journals and e-Content.
  • To maintain effective links with all the College staff, let them understand and respond to the challenges in education and approaches to learning.
