DEPARTMENT OF MECHANICAL ENGINEERING

Welcome to Department

The Department of Mechanical Engineering was established in the year 2010. The department offers both B. Tech and M. Tech courses. The postgraduate program, M. Tech in Computational Mechanics in Mechanical Engineering was started in the year 2016. The annual intake for B. Tech and M. Tech courses are 120+ and 18 students respectively.

Department has 14 well equipped laboratories contains very advanced equipments including Wire EDM, 3D printer, CMM, CNC, Universal Milling, Robotic Arm, IC engines which enable the students to gain hands on experience of handling basic equipments to advance equipments. The department has well established Design and Simulation Laboratory equipped with 90 high end configuration systems which support the software packages such as Auto CAD, CATIA, SOLIDWORKS, ANSYS, MATLAB, Deform 3D, Fusion 360 and Inventor which help the students to sharpen their designing skills. Department also contains library, innovation lab, research lab.

Vision

To develop and serve practicing engineers, industry, government, educational and technical societies through consultancy work by providing professional expertise.

Mission

Impart quality education and training to nurture globally competitive mechanical engineers. Provide vital state of the art research facilities to create, interpret, apply and disseminate knowledge.
